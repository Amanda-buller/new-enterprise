Behind the Idea

Why LamiCare Was Founded As a horse owner, I personally experienced the stress and heartbreak of managing a horse with laminitis. Every grazing decision felt like a gamble. I was constantly second-guessing myself — unsure whether the paddock was safe, or if the sugar levels were too high. Traditional Brix testing, while valuable, was time-consuming, technical, and hard to maintain regularly. That's when the idea for LamiCare was born. I didn't just want a tool that could read sugar levels — I needed a solution that simplified the entire grazing decision process, reduced risk, and gave peace of mind to horse owners like me..
